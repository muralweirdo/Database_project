About Us

We're students of FAST NUCES, currently in our 4th semester. This is our Database Project. This took a whole lot of time for us to design and implement so we hope you'll like this. Also in this trying times of COVID-19, we hope you stay safe!

This has been a very learning and exciting experience for us, as a team and on an individual level too. This project is to be submitted to Mr. Mehmood Munir, who is our TA in this course.

What is this project about?

This is our version of a basic ART Gallary Management System. Serious and successful art businesses run on a great amount of detailed information. How accurately the information is recorded, how clearly it is organized, and how efficiently it is integrated is critical to success.

This project consists of a lot of interesting features including saving the details of various new artis, buyers, the artwork produced and even a mechanism of purchasing art.
